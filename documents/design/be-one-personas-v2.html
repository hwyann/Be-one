Left column: objectives linked directly to a KR (solid pill). Right column: objectives linked at the objective level (dashed pill). Physical grouping = instant visual count of how many of each type you have.

Why Option A works: categorical grouping — a KR-link is a stronger commitment (measurable target) than objective-level. Seeing them side by side makes the balance obvious. Scans well when you have 5–15 objectives.
Weakness: loses the connection to the parent company objective — you have to read the "↳" text.

Option B — Grouped under parent company objective, with cascading branches · ✅ CHOSEN

Each company objective is a container. KR-linked individuals nest under the specific KR (solid connector). Objective-level individuals attach with a dashed connector at the bottom of the group. You see the whole tree in one glance.
